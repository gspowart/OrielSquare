Who We Are

Oriel Square is a trusted partner to the most successful education companies in the world. Our team has a high level of publishing and education that enables us to deliver excellence in strategy, research and publishing projects.

We maintain the best network of education professionals in the industry so that we can always offer you specialism, whatever the subject or format.

What We Do

An integrated approach to educational publishing is the foundation of all our work. We provide services to support education organisations throughout the whole of the strategy, research and publishing cycle, in the UK and worldwide.
